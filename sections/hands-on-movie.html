Hands-on 2

Movie dataset

  • Partial dump of themoviedb.org database
  • Contains 12k movies and 50k actors and directors

Assignment:

  • Find the Director that has directed most movies
  • Find the Movie that has had the most contributing Persons
  • Create relationships between Persons that have contributed together to the same movie
  • Add count properties to the Persons telling the amount of Persons they have worked together with
  • Obtain a top 10 of Persons with the highest counts

Database available at http://mazerunnern.cloudapp.net:7474

Hint: Cypher has functions, and count might be one of them
